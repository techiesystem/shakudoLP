1. Learning Architecture: The Shakudo Enablement Pyramid

Think of Shakudo's Stack learning as a progressive capability pyramid, starting with broad awareness and narrowing to expert mastery.

Awareness & Conceptual Fluency
Functional Proficiency
Operational Mastery
Solution & Architecture Design
Expert & Advocate

How the Inverted Pyramid Works

The Shakudo Enablement Pyramid represents a capability funnel that starts with broad awareness (widest at the top) and narrows to specialized expertise (narrowest at the bottom). Each level builds upon the previous, creating a progressive journey from initial understanding to deep mastery.

1

Awareness & Conceptual Fluency

Prospects, Exec Sponsors, Pre-Sales

Foundational understanding of Shakudo's value proposition, platform capabilities, and business impact. Entry point for all users.

Outcome: "I understand what Shakudo does and why it matters."
2

Functional Proficiency

Data Scientists, ML Engineers, Platform Users

Hands-on skills to effectively use the platform for core tasks and workflows. Focus on practical application.

Outcome: "I can use the platform to get real work done."
3

Operational Mastery

Platform Admins, ML Ops, Data Platform Teams

Advanced skills to manage, optimize, and govern Shakudo deployments at scale. Moving from user to operator.

Outcome: "I can run, scale, and govern this platform."
4

Solution & Architecture Design

Architects, Partners, Senior Engineers

Strategic ability to design end-to-end solutions and architect implementations. Creating with Shakudo.

Outcome: "I can design solutions with Shakudo."
5

Expert & Advocate

Champions, Partners, Internal SMEs

Mastery to teach, optimize, and evangelize Shakudo's capabilities. The pinnacle of platform expertise.

Outcome: "I can teach, optimize, and sell Shakudo."

3. Hands-On Labs: Real-World, Progressive, Irreversible

Labs are where credibility is earned through scenario-driven, progressive learning experiences.

Lab Design Principles

Scenario-driven
Real company, real problem
Progressive difficulty
Build skills incrementally
Irreversible steps
No copy-paste success
Observable outcomes
Measurable skill validation

Sample Lab Progression (Data → ML → Ops)

1

Getting Productive Fast

"You've joined a data science team. Your environment is inconsistent and onboarding is slow."

  • Create workspace
  • Configure environment
  • Access shared data
  • Run first experiment
Skill Check

Environment reproducibility - Can recreate identical workspace from scratch

2

Scaling Experimentation

"Your team needs to run systematic experiments with proper tracking and comparison."

  • Parameter sweeps
  • Experiment tracking
  • Compare runs
  • Optimize hyperparameters
Skill Check

Experiment lineage & metrics - Track and compare multiple experiment runs

3

Production Handoff

"Your model shows promising results and needs to be prepared for production deployment."

  • Package model
  • Register artifact
  • Hand off to ML Ops pipeline
  • Validate deployment readiness
Skill Check

Model readiness checklist - Complete all steps for production deployment

4

Operational Reality

"Your deployed model needs monitoring, drift detection, and potential retraining."

  • Deploy model
  • Monitor drift
  • Trigger retraining
  • Update production model
Skill Check

Operational decision-making - Determine when and how to retrain based on metrics

Lab Deliverables

✅ Checkpoints with validation criteria
📊 Logs, metrics, artifacts
💭 "Explain what happened" reflection prompts
🏆 Completion certificates
